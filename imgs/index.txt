The format for the main character g is:

g<state><frame><direction>.png


<state>:

c: Crouching
cr: Crawling/second frame of dying
da: Dying 1
db: Dying 2
dc: Dead
fa: Falling (fast)
fb: Falling (slow)
ja: Jumping (fast)
jb: Jumping (slow)
k: Kneeling/first frame of dying
r: Running
rs: Sliding while running
rt: Turning while running
s: Still
